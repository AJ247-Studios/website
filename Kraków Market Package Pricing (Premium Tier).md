
- **Sports Packages:** Local sports event coverage (photo + short video) typically starts at **1,000–2,500 PLN** for a 3–4 hour shoot[nowelski.pl](https://nowelski.pl/fotograf-cennik/#:~:text=,cio%20godzinny)[flypix.pl](https://www.flypix.pl/cennik-uslug/#:~:text=). Upgrading to a 5+ hour “premium” sports package (roughly 400+ edited images and a multi-minute highlight reel) pushes the price into the **~3,000–4,500 PLN** range[flypix.pl](https://www.flypix.pl/cennik-uslug/#:~:text=)[charles-studio.pl](https://charles-studio.pl/en/wedding-videographer-wedding-filming-krakow/#:~:text=The%20costs%20of%20wedding%20videography,the%20details%2C%20which%20will%20allow). (For example, a combined photo+video package for ~4 h of event coverage is listed at ~4,500 PLN[flypix.pl](https://www.flypix.pl/cennik-uslug/#:~:text=Pakiet%20Foto%20%2B%20Video).)
    
- **Concert Packages:** Basic concert coverage (≈4 h, ~250 photos, ~1 min highlight) is usually on the order of **1,500–3,000 PLN**. Premium concert packages (6+ h, ~500 edited photos, ~5 min video, USB drive and prints) command **~4,000–6,000 PLN**. For instance, high-end event photo+video packages in Kraków start around **4,500 PLN** for 4 h[flypix.pl](https://www.flypix.pl/cennik-uslug/#:~:text=Pakiet%20Foto%20%2B%20Video), and “VIP” all-day concert packages (two photographers, drone shots, etc.) can reach **~4,000 PLN** or more[aofoto.pl](https://aofoto.pl/fotografia-eventowa-krakow/#:~:text=Pakiet%20VIP).
    
- **Wedding Packages:** Upper-tier wedding coverage in Kraków typically runs **5,000–6,500 PLN** for full-day photo+video service. (One leading photographer’s “Premium” all-day package – including 750 photos and a 5-minute film – is **5,600 PLN**[wojciechgruszczykfotografia.pl](https://wojciechgruszczykfotografia.pl/?page_id=32#:~:text=Cena%202025%3A%205600%20z%C5%82).) More modest 6-hour packages with ~300–400 edited photos and a brief highlight video generally cost **3,000–4,000 PLN**[nowelski.pl](https://nowelski.pl/fotograf-cennik/#:~:text=,sesji)[charles-studio.pl](https://charles-studio.pl/en/wedding-videographer-wedding-filming-krakow/#:~:text=The%20costs%20of%20wedding%20videography,the%20details%2C%20which%20will%20allow). In other words, _Standard_ 6 h wedding packages (~300 images, 2 min video) run in the low-PLN-thousands[nowelski.pl](https://nowelski.pl/fotograf-cennik/#:~:text=,sesji)[charles-studio.pl](https://charles-studio.pl/en/wedding-videographer-wedding-filming-krakow/#:~:text=The%20costs%20of%20wedding%20videography,the%20details%2C%20which%20will%20allow), while _Premium_ full-day packages (e.g. 10+ min edit, album, USB) approach **5–6 k PLN**[wojciechgruszczykfotografia.pl](https://wojciechgruszczykfotografia.pl/?page_id=32#:~:text=Cena%202025%3A%205600%20z%C5%82)[charles-studio.pl](https://charles-studio.pl/en/wedding-videographer-wedding-filming-krakow/#:~:text=The%20costs%20of%20wedding%20videography,the%20details%2C%20which%20will%20allow).
    
- **Photo Session Packages:** Portrait or promo shoots are commonly priced by time and image count. A **1–2 hour session** (e.g. business headshots or personal branding) with ~10–30 final images costs roughly **400–600 PLN**[mariuszlenart.pl](https://mariuszlenart.pl/fotografia-portretowa-oferta/#:~:text=Pakiet%20MINI%20400%20z%C5%82)[halofoto.pl](https://halofoto.pl/pl.fotografia_portretowa_cena.html#:~:text=FOTOGRAFIA%20PORTRETOWA%20). Extended sessions (2–3 h) with ~50+ final images – often including makeup, multiple outfits and prints – go up to **800–1,000+ PLN**[mariuszlenart.pl](https://mariuszlenart.pl/fotografia-portretowa-oferta/#:~:text=Pakiet%20VIP%20950%20z%C5%82)[halofoto.pl](https://halofoto.pl/pl.fotografia_portretowa_cena.html#:~:text=FOTOGRAFIA%20PORTRETOWA%20). For example, a Kraków studio offers a 1.5 h session with 10 edited portraits for 600 PLN[mariuszlenart.pl](https://mariuszlenart.pl/fotografia-portretowa-oferta/#:~:text=Pakiet%20STANDARD%20600%20z%C5%82), while premium “VIP” sessions (3 h, 15 images, pro makeup) are ~950 PLN[mariuszlenart.pl](https://mariuszlenart.pl/fotografia-portretowa-oferta/#:~:text=Pakiet%20VIP%20950%20z%C5%82). Business/CV photo shoots start around **400–500 PLN**[vravell.com](https://www.vravell.com/pakiety-i-ceny#:~:text=), and high-end branding sessions (2+ people, 800+ PLN) are also common[vravell.com](https://www.vravell.com/pakiety-i-ceny#:~:text=Fotografia%20Portretowa%20Krak%C3%B3w).
    

## Additional / Corporate & Commercial Packages

_Corporate and conference events in Kraków (e.g. at venues like the ICE Kraków) require professional coverage. Pricing for corporate events is similar to large-scale event packages:_

- **Corporate Events (photo/video):** A 3-hour photo-only event package (100–150 images) runs around **800–1,200 PLN**[nowelski.pl](https://nowelski.pl/fotograf-cennik/#:~:text=,cio%20godzinny). A full day (6–8 h) event reportorial package is roughly **1,500–2,500 PLN** (photo only)[nowelski.pl](https://nowelski.pl/fotograf-cennik/#:~:text=,cio%20godzinny). Adding video (1–2 camera operators + short highlight edit) raises this into the **4,000–5,000 PLN**+ range[flypix.pl](https://www.flypix.pl/cennik-uslug/#:~:text=)[flypix.pl](https://www.flypix.pl/cennik-uslug/#:~:text=Pakiet%20Foto%20%2B%20Video). Many Kraków vendors bundle photo+video – FlyPix, for example, quotes **≈4,500 PLN** for a 4 h photo+video combo[flypix.pl](https://www.flypix.pl/cennik-uslug/#:~:text=Pakiet%20Foto%20%2B%20Video).
    
- **Business Portraits / Headshots:** Studio business portrait sessions (~1–2 h, basic editing) typically start at **500 PLN**[vravell.com](https://www.vravell.com/pakiety-i-ceny#:~:text=) (e.g. corporate headshots, LinkedIn profiles). Larger corporate packages (multiple employees photographed) run proportionally higher (often **from ~900 PLN** for a small team)[vravell.com](https://www.vravell.com/pakiety-i-ceny#:~:text=).
    
- **Product/Commercial Photography:** Package pricing is often per image or fixed bundles. For instance, a professional studio offers a **“Packshot” 10-photo package** (2 h session) for **≈615 PLN**[digitalcity.com.pl](https://digitalcity.com.pl/produkt/zdjecia-produktowe-packshot-pakiet-nr-1-10-zdjec/#:~:text=615), 20 images for ~922 PLN[digitalcity.com.pl](https://digitalcity.com.pl/produkt/zdjecia-produktowe-packshot-pakiet-nr-1-10-zdjec/#:~:text=Zdj%C4%99cia%20produktowe%20%28packshot%29%20,30%20zdj%C4%99%C4%87), etc. Complex campaigns (lookbooks, ads, or video ads) are quoted case-by-case, but expect basic product shoots to scale linearly (hundreds of PLN per image, 600+ PLN per 10-image set[digitalcity.com.pl](https://digitalcity.com.pl/produkt/zdjecia-produktowe-packshot-pakiet-nr-1-10-zdjec/#:~:text=615)).
    
- **Add-Ons:** Premium packages often include or allow add-ons such as drone footage, albums, extra prints, etc. For example, the above 6–8 h “VIP” event package includes drone photography by default[aofoto.pl](https://aofoto.pl/fotografia-eventowa-krakow/#:~:text=Pakiet%20VIP). Separately, Kraków providers typically charge **~500–1,500 PLN** extra for drone video/photo coverage, or for luxury add-ons like custom albums and extended color grading.
    

## Custom & Tailored Packages

Clients can mix services to create custom bundles. For example, extending any package by extra hours, adding a second photographer, ordering additional edited photos, or combining event and portrait shoots can all be arranged. Pricing for custom packages is determined on a case-by-case basis, usually starting from a base hourly rate (**~1,000+ PLN per additional hour of coverage**) plus fees for extra deliverables. In practice, premium vendors will quote custom needs after consultation, but the above rates provide a market benchmark.
